About Me

I am currently an Master student at Guangzhou University, advised by Prof. Xinchang Zhang. Prior to this, I received my B.E. degree from Northeast Agricultural University. My current research focuses on Generative Models and Multi-modal Image Generation. Specifically, I am deeply interested in exploring the application of advanced deep learning techniques (such as flow matching and diffusion models) to remote sensing imagery for tasks like building extraction. I am always open to research discussions and collaborations. Please feel free to contact me.
